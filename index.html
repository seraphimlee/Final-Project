ATEEZ

8 Makes 1 Team

On October 24, 2018 ATEEZ made their debut under the management of KQ Entertainment, who was actually more known for their music producers rather than idols but overall was still a relatively small company. As the first official idols of the company, the 8-member team took to the stage with two debut songs: 'Treasure' and 'Pirate King" from their album 'TREASURE EP.1: All to Zero'. They later went on to win their first music show award with their comeback, 'Wave' the next summer of 2019. The members consist of Hongjoong, Seonghwa, Yunho, Yeosang, San, Mingi, Wooyoung, and Jongho. Their fandom is known as ATINY, a combination of ATEEZ and DESTINY. By the end of the year, they received the 'Worldwide Fans' Choice' award at the MAMA awards and were about to embark on their second world tour before COVID-19 hit. Nevertheless, their success is unquestionable and while their talents speak for themselves, they are also a very down-to-earth, humble group of best friends.
